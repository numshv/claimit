# Kartu Prakerja

## 1. Description
Kartu Prakerja is a government-funded skill development program managed by the Management Committee (Manajemen Pelaksana) under Indonesia's Coordinating Ministry for Economic Affairs. It combines access to online training courses with a cash incentive paid upon completion. It is designed for job seekers and informal workers, not as a welfare transfer — the emphasis is on training-first, incentive-second.

## 2. Core Eligibility Requirements
- **Age:** Must be at least 18 years old.
- **Education status:** Not currently enrolled in formal education (school or university).
- **Employment status:** Unemployed, recently laid off, or an informal/gig worker wanting to upskill. Formally employed workers may apply but are lower priority in the selection algorithm.
- **Citizenship:** Indonesian citizen (WNI) with a valid KTP.
- **Digital access:** Must have an active phone number and a bank account or e-wallet (OVO, GoPay, DANA, BNI, BRI, Mandiri) to receive incentives.

## 3. Exclusions and Conflicts (CRITICAL)
- **Who cannot apply:** Active government employees (ASN), TNI, and Polri. Directors and commissioners of BUMN/BUMD. Members of the DPR, DPD, and DPRD (national and regional legislators). Current PKH recipients. People who successfully received Prakerja benefits in a previous batch.
- **Program Conflicts:** Prakerja **cannot** be combined with PKH. The selection system cross-checks against the PKH beneficiary database and automatically disqualifies current PKH recipients.
- **Re-application:** Once successfully receiving Prakerja in one batch, applicants are permanently disqualified from applying again, even in future years.

## 4. Benefits (Pros)
- Training voucher of **Rp 3,500,000** redeemable for accredited online courses on partner platforms (Skill Academy, Tokopedia, Karier.mu, Pijar Mahir, and others).
- Cash completion incentive of **Rp 600,000** disbursed after finishing a qualifying course.
- Survey incentive of **Rp 50,000** per survey (maximum 3 surveys = Rp 150,000).
- Certified completion credentials that are recognized by many employers for entry-level roles.

## 5. Application Steps & Required Documents
- **Step 1:** Register an account at prakerja.go.id using your KTP number, name, date of birth, and active phone number for OTP verification.
- **Step 2:** Complete the mandatory pre-selection test (skills assessment including reading comprehension and basic numeracy). A higher score improves selection chances.
- **Step 3:** Wait for the batch announcement on your dashboard. If not selected, you may re-apply in the next open batch.
- **Step 4:** Once selected, choose and purchase a course from a partner training platform using your voucher balance.
- **Step 5:** Complete at least the minimum required learning hours, then fill in the post-training survey to unlock your cash incentive.
- **Documents Needed:**
  - KTP (valid Indonesian national ID) — used for identity and eligibility cross-check
  - Active mobile phone number — for OTP and incentive notifications
  - Bank account or e-wallet — for incentive disbursement (OVO, GoPay, DANA, BNI, BRI, or Mandiri)
  - NPWP (optional — required only if total incentive exceeds the annual income tax threshold)
