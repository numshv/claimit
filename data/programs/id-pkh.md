# PKH (Program Keluarga Harapan)

## 1. Description
PKH is Indonesia's flagship conditional cash transfer (CCT) program, managed by the Ministry of Social Affairs (Kemensos). Unlike unconditional handouts, payments are tied to fulfillment of health and education requirements. Funds are paid quarterly via the Kartu Keluarga Sejahtera (KKS) card. The program targets households in the DTKS (Data Terpadu Kesejahteraan Sosial) — Indonesia's national welfare registry.

## 2. Core Eligibility Requirements
- **DTKS Registration:** Household must be registered in DTKS (checked at cekbansos.kemensos.go.id). This is a hard prerequisite — no exceptions.
- **Qualifying Component:** At least one of the following must be present in the household:
  - Children aged 0–5 years old
  - Pregnant or breastfeeding women
  - Children enrolled in SD (primary), SMP (junior high), or SMA (senior high)
  - Elderly members aged 70 years or older
  - Family members with severe disabilities
- **Income Level:** No formal income ceiling, but practical targeting means households must be in the bottom 40% of income distribution.
- **Citizenship:** Must be Indonesian citizens (WNI) with valid KTP and KK.

## 3. Exclusions and Conflicts (CRITICAL)
- **Who cannot apply:** Families not in DTKS. Active government employees (ASN), TNI, and Polri members and their spouses. Families already receiving Kartu Prakerja benefits.
- **Program Conflicts:** PKH **cannot** be combined with Kartu Prakerja. The Prakerja selection algorithm automatically screens out active PKH recipients. Receiving both simultaneously results in termination of one benefit.
- **Suspension triggers:** Failure to attend P2K2 (family development) meetings, or failure to meet health/education conditions (e.g., missed child immunizations, school dropout) leads to temporary suspension.

## 4. Benefits (Pros)
- Quarterly cash transfers with component-based amounts:
  - Children 0–5 years: Rp 3,000,000/year
  - Pregnant/breastfeeding women: Rp 3,000,000/year
  - SD students: Rp 900,000/year
  - SMP students: Rp 1,500,000/year
  - SMA students: Rp 2,000,000/year
  - Elderly 70+: Rp 2,400,000/year
  - Severe disability: Rp 2,400,000/year
- Automatic eligibility for BPJS Kesehatan PBI (free health insurance).
- Can be combined with BPNT/Sembako food assistance and PIP education grants.

## 5. Application Steps & Required Documents
- **Step 1:** Check DTKS status at cekbansos.kemensos.go.id using KTP number.
- **Step 2 (if not in DTKS):** Visit RT/RW chief and request inclusion in the next DTKS update cycle (musdes/muskel). This is mandatory before any application.
- **Step 3:** Once in DTKS, a local Pendamping PKH (assigned social worker) will contact eligible households. Alternatively, visit the nearest Dinas Sosial office proactively.
- **Step 4:** Attend mandatory P2K2 meetings regularly to maintain benefit status.
- **Documents Needed:**
  - KTP for both spouses (if married)
  - Kartu Keluarga (KK) — household registry card
  - Akta Kelahiran (birth certificates) for each child component
  - Buku KIA or immunization record for children under 6
  - School enrollment proof (Surat Keterangan Aktif Sekolah) for school-age children
